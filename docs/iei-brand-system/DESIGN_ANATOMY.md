## 🌟 CROSS-PIECE PATTERNS (what 22 designs reveal collectively)

### Patterns that recur in BOTH Tab's work AND her saves
- **Cutout portrait isolation** — subjects extracted from backgrounds, placed on designed canvases (not photos-in-rectangles)
- **Bold caps display typography taking up real space** — Inter Black / Bebas / Anton family
- **Drippy/oozy interior on bold type** — POC Queer + Why Deny (Tab's own) + the "drippy" descriptor Tab uses repeatedly
- **Patterns/textures as subtle storytelling** — hexagons (tech), dots (heritage), peanuts (BR), pixels (music), stars (vintage Black culture)
- **Quote marks AS graphic elements** — sized like art, not punctuation (James Brown · TLC Press Release)
- **Real Black/POC faces centered** — across ~95% of all 22 designs
- **Co-branding handled in dedicated zones** — Cohado on BR pieces · partner logos clean horizontal rows

### 5 aesthetic clusters that emerged
1. **Activist Community Recruiting** — maximalist sticker-collage, recruiting energy, DIY zine feel (POC Queer, BLK ASS pieces)
2. **Type-as-Art Custom Letterforms** — custom typography that IS the design (Artscape, ROOT WORD, PACK LIGHT)
3. **Vintage Diaspora Poster** — warm earth palettes, African pattern references, script alongside chunky display (Backyard Bashment, Spirit World)
4. **Editorial Density Done Right** — info-rich done elegantly (Gut Experience, Jasmin Manning + calendar)
5. **Pattern-as-Branding Signature** — distinctive recurring texture (Honey Dijon pixels)

### New Tab design vocab (added to BRAND_VOICE_SPEC.md)
**drippy · hypnotic · dope ass realness · let's go everybody's gathering · random as shit (positive) · boom in your face · real flow real groovy · cinematic street pop art · Afro Pop Futuristic · dope ass show · real shit I would look at · we got our shit together · THE FLINT (speaker naming) · jagged · oozy · emo-a-little-bit**

### A1 — DUMI × TLC "NEW RELEASE" (streetwear campaign drop)
**Context:** DUMI Apparel × TLC clothing campaign for "The Loyalty Collection" — part of a multi-piece campaign suite (some pieces flash/move/meme-energy; this one chosen as RAW anchor)
**Palette:** Black `#0A0A0A` · Charcoal crumpled-paper `#2A2A2A` · Orange-Red `#E94F2A` · White cream `#F2EBDC`
**Typography:** Heavy condensed sans-serif (Bebas/Anton family) in orange-red caps for "NEW RELEASE" · script lowercase "TLC" overlay · DUMI infinity wordmark logo lockup
**Layout:** Asymmetric edge-to-edge with diagonal hierarchy — headline upper-left, logo upper-right, product photography mid-frame, copy/URL lower-left, decorative TLC bottom-right
**Photo:** Product photography cutouts mounted on torn-paper white squares, double-shadow layered, slight rotation
**Decorative:** Crumpled black paper bg texture · chevron arrows `>>>` after "NEW" · torn-paper edges on photo mounts · TLC handwritten script floating · newspaper clipping snippets bottom-right
**Tab's why:** *"Urban realness, freshness, dopeness, real in your face. Loyalty in the streets — we're loyal to each other. The win: it FLOWS when all assets are seen together."*
**Signature moves:** Torn-paper photo mounts with double shadows · chevron-arrow as directional accent after action words · script-on-grunge as collab-marker · campaign-suite cohesion (this static piece sits alongside animated meme/GIF pieces)
**AI lesson:** Streetwear drops need a STATIC RAW anchor + ANIMATED LOUD variants. One brand, multiple energy registers per campaign. Real-paper textures elevate raw.
