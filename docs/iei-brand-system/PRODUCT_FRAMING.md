**Author:** Synthesized from Tab Wolod's own facilitator guide, participant workbook, and 10 worksheets (see `source/`).
**Date:** 2026-05-18.

---

## What we're building

A **digital, AI-assisted, self-serve version of Tab Wolod's proprietary live workshop: "The IEI Brand System."**

This is NOT "an AI brand kit generator." This is **Tab's signature methodology productized.** Every screen, every prompt, every output reflects Tab's framework, voice, and language — not a generic LLM brand-deck workflow.

The live workshop runs ~4 hours and produces, for the participant: a Brilliant Idea, a brand identity, an ideal client definition, a sellable offer, a brand narrative, a content plan, and the first 5 social posts. The digital MVP does the same thing — async, AI-coached, in their own time, with downloadable assets.

## The IEI Brand System — the 6 modules

This is the product spine. Every screen in the UI maps to one of these modules.

| # | Module | What it produces | Timing (live) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **One Brilliant Idea** | The locked-in "I love X + I'm good at Y = brilliant idea Z" anchor | 45 min |
| 2 | **Who Are You?** (ABCs — Authentic Brand Creation) | Identity, Story, Mission, Voice, Secret Sauce — plus mission statement, vision, 3-5 core values, 3 tagline options, 3 IG bio options | ~60 min |
| 3 | **Who Are They?** | Ideal client: Demographics (Layer 1), Psychographics (Layer 2), Dream 100 Lite (Layer 3) | ~45 min |
| 4 | **What Do They Need?** | The 6-part offering framework + value-based pricing + "I offer [X] for [Y], that helps them [Z]" statement | ~45 min |
| 5 | **The Narrative** | 7-part story (Origin · Why · Struggle · Turning Point · Who You Serve · How You Help · The Invitation) + Hook Line + First 5 Posts content map | ~45 min |
| 6 | **Content & Communications** | The 5-Post Framework written out (Awareness 50% · Trust 30% · Convert 20%) + Master AI Prompt for ongoing content + Brand Cheat Sheet | ~30 min |

Every module ends with a **"LOCK IT IN"** commitment ritual — a single sentence or statement the user owns going forward. This is non-negotiable UX: every module has a Lock It In screen.

## The Fourfold Path to Meaningful Marketing (Worksheet 1 — the master GTM)

After the 6 modules produce the brand, Tab's "Fourfold Path" governs how the brand goes to market over **6 weeks**. The MVP may not deliver all four phases as automated outputs, but the brand playbook must MAP TO this path so the user sees the full road.

| Phase | Weeks | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Step 1 — Ideation** | Weeks 1-2 | Brand identity, SMART goals, mission/vision/values, voice, brand style guide (logo, colors, type), associated assets (website, social templates, business cards, presentations) |
| **Step 2 — Impact** | Weeks 3-4 | Market research (competitor analysis WS6), customer persona (WS7), product positioning (WS8), marketing strategy + channel selection + Dream 100 (WS9, WS10) |
| **Step 3 — Implementation** | Week 5 | Content development, keyword research, content pillars, content calendar, content production across channels |
| **Step 4 — Income** | Weeks 5-6 | Sales strategy, pricing, CRM, revenue forecasting, pre-launch checklist |

## Tab's superpower (the differentiator)

> *"I can have a conversation with someone without jargon to help people get clear on what they do. I can sit with you, listen deeply, and pull out the gold you've been too close to see."*

**Implication for the product:** the AI's job is NOT to generate brand assets from a form. The AI's job is to BE Tab in conversation — pull the gold out via real-feeling questions, surface what the user can't see in themselves, and produce the brand from THAT.
