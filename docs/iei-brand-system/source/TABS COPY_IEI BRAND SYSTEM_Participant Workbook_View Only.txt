MODULE 04

What Do They Need?

Creating Your Offering — From Idea to Sellable Package







Price reflects the TRANSFORMATION — not the time. Not the session. The result of all of it. If your offering gives someone a system they can use for the rest of their business life — what is THAT worth? Use the 6-part framework to build one clear, specific, sellable offering.



THE 6-PART OFFERING FRAMEWORK

Six questions. When you can answer all six — you have an offering. Not just an idea. Something real that someone can say yes to.

1

Who is it for?



2

What problem does it solve?



3

What IS it? (format · length · delivery)



4

What does it take YOU to deliver? (your inputs)



5

What are the DELIVERABLES? List everything.



6

What is the OUTCOME? The transformation.

Value-based pricing: price based on the transformation, not the time.

Your story is your strategy.







7 parts: Origin · Why · Struggle · Turning Point · Who You Serve · How You Help · The Invitation. You don't tell all 7 in every post — you pick one chapter. Over time people piece together the whole story — and come to know you, trust you, and buy.

PART 3 — WHY YOUR OFFER IS THE SOLUTION
