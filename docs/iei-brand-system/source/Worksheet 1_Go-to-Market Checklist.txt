Worksheet 1: The FourFold Path to Meaningful Marketing Go-to-Market Checklist



Use this checklist to guide your go-to-market efforts and ensure that each phase of "The Fourfold Path to Meaningful Marketing" is executed effectively to drive meaningful impact and income for your brand or product.



Step 1 - Ideation (Weeks 1 & 2)

Brand Identity Development:

Create a list of SMART Goals (Worksheet 5)

Define brand values, mission, and vision statement. Develop a brand voice and tone that resonates with your target audience. (Worksheet 2)

Complete Brand Messaging [AI prompts] (Worksheet 3)

Create a brand style guide outlining visual elements such as logos, colors, and typography. (Worksheet 4)

Develop associated assets such as: website, social media templates, presentations, business cards, social media covers, etc. (In Progress)





Step 2 - Impact (Weeks 3 & 4)

Market Research:

Competitor Analysis (Worksheet 6)

Customer Persona Development:

Create detailed customer personas based on demographic, psychographic, and behavioral data. (Worksheet 7)

Product Positioning:

Articulate the unique value proposition of your product or service, identify key benefits and features. (Worksheet 8)

Marketing Strategy Development:

Define & organize marketing objectives, target audience, and key messages (Meaningful Marketing Matrix).

Conduct extensive market research to drill down on Niche Markets (Worksheet 9) and Dream Customers (Worksheet 10)

Determine marketing channels and tactics based on audience preferences and behavior (Dream 100 Exercise - Meaningful Marketing Spreadsheet)



Step 3 - Implementation (Week 5)  

Content Development:

Complete Keyword Research and Brainstorm Content Ideas

Create Content Pillars and Graphic Templates

Develop a content calendar outlining topics, formats, and publication schedule.

Produce a variety of content formats such as blog posts, videos, infographics, and social media posts.

Ensure content is optimized for search engines and tailored to each marketing channel.

Finalize associated assets such as: website, social media templates, presentations, business cards, social media covers, etc. (In Progress)

Channel Selection/Distribution Platform:

Identify primary and secondary marketing channels based on audience reach and engagement.

Develop channel-specific strategies and tactics to maximize visibility and engagement.



Step 4 - Income (Week 5)

Sales and Distribution Strategy:

Determine sales channels and distribution partners to reach target audience effectively.

Develop sales collateral and materials to support sales efforts.

Implement a customer relationship management (CRM) system to track sales leads and conversions.

Pricing Strategy:

Conduct pricing research to understand market dynamics and competitive pricing.

Set pricing tiers and options based on value proposition and target customer segments.

Monitor pricing trends and adjust pricing strategy as needed to remain competitive.

Revenue Forecasting:

Estimate revenue projections based on sales forecasts, pricing strategy, and market trends.

Monitor sales performance and revenue metrics regularly to track progress against targets.

Adjust revenue forecasts and business plans based on actual performance and market conditions.

Prep for Launch (Week 6)

Complete Pre Launch Activities
