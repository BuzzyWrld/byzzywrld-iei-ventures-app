# First 90 Days — Financial Model

**Purpose:** A realistic revenue + cost projection for Brand Blueprint's first 90 days. Informs bankroll decisions (revenue-funded vs. raise), hiring timing, and price-change triggers.

**Inputs (locked decisions):**
- Pricing tiers: $997 / $1,997 / $3,997
- Tab bandwidth: 20 hrs/week (sales + review + intake)
- Tab-in-loop concierge for all Stage 1 customers
- GTM motion: one-to-one sales (Stage 1) days 1-30 · cohort prep (Stage 2) days 31-60 · cohort sales (Stage 2) days 61-90

**Caveat:** All numbers are estimates. Actual results depend on outreach quality, conversion rate, market timing. Update this doc monthly with actuals.

## When to make pricing changes

Triggers to RAISE prices:

- ✅ NPS rolling average ≥ 9
- ✅ Capacity consistently 90%+ booked
- ✅ Outreach conversion ≥ 25%
- ✅ Refund rate < 5%

If 3 of 4 hit by week 6 → raise basic to $1,297, content to $2,497, full to $4,997.

Triggers to LOWER prices (or add lower-priced entry):

- ❌ Conversion < 10% (people seeing landing, not buying)
- ❌ Frequent "I love this but it's outside my budget" feedback
- ❌ Refund requests citing "didn't realize how much it was"

If 2 of 3 hit by week 4 → add a $497 "Brand Starter" (subset of Basic — just messaging + logo) as funnel entry to Basic.

## When to hire help

**VA (10-15 hrs/week, ~$25-40/hr, ~$1,500/mo):**
Hire when: Tab's hours hit 25/week+ sustainably for 2 consecutive weeks.
Role: respond to email, schedule calls, package deliverables, send delivery emails.

**Junior brand designer (5-10 hrs/week, ~$50-75/hr, ~$2,000/mo):**
Hire when: 5+ customers/week for 2 consecutive weeks.
Role: refine AI-generated logos, customize Canva templates, polish brand kit pages.

**Dev (part-time, project basis, ~$3,000-8,000 total):**
Hire when: Stage 2 cohort requires the customer dashboard + automation per `DEV_HANDOFF.md` days 8-30 work.
Role: chat intake UI, synthesis bridge, Stripe webhook auto-provisioning.

## Three things this model assumes (and the risks)

1. **20 hrs/week is sustainable for Tab.** Truth check: it's not, indefinitely. Realistic plan = 20 hrs/week for first 60 days, then hire VA, then Tab drops to 12-15 hrs/week on the highest-leverage activities (sales, premium reviews, cohort facilitation).
2. **First 5 customers leave great testimonials.** This is the linchpin. Bad first 5 = no word of mouth = projections fail. Tab-in-loop quality bar matters most in weeks 1-3.
3. **Outreach + landing converts as projected.** First 30 days will tell. If conversion is half this model's assumption, time-to-revenue doubles but doesn't break the business — just delays Stage 2 by ~30 days.

## Sanity check vs. Tab's $550/hr 1:1 consulting rate

Tab currently charges $550/hr for Brand-Storming. At 20 billable hours/week, that's $44,000/month gross — IF she could fill every hour with billable work, which she can't (sales, ops, content all unbilled).

Brand Blueprint at 20 hrs/week generates ~$21,000-$27,000/month gross — LESS per hour worked.

**So why bother?** Three reasons:
1. **Leverage.** AI does the heavy lifting. Tab's hours go further (4 customers in 20 hrs vs. 2 customers via pure 1:1).
2. **Productized = saleable later.** Brand Blueprint can be hired-out, white-labeled, or sold as a business. $550/hr consulting can't.
3. **Stage 2 (cohorts) + Stage 4 (licensing) unlock far higher economics.** First 90 days is just the foundation. Cohorts at $1,500/seat × 10 seats/cohort × 1 cohort/month = $15K/month with Tab spending 5-8 hrs total. That's $2,000+/hr Tab time.

This model is intentionally conservative for the first 90 days because **the goal isn't max revenue — it's max validation.** Real revenue scale comes in months 4-12.
