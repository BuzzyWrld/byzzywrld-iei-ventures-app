## 2. What We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

### a. Information You Provide Directly
- **Account / Order Information:** name, email address, business name, phone number (optional), billing address.
- **Payment Information:** processed by our payment processor, Stripe, Inc. We do not store your full credit-card number on our servers; Stripe stores it and provides us with a token plus the last 4 digits and card brand for receipts and dispute handling.
- **Intake Information:** During the intake call or in-app intake, you share information about your business, your story, your goals, your audience, your products/services, your inspirations, and your brand preferences. This may include personal information about you (your background, your "why") and information about your business operations.
- **Communications:** any emails, messages, or scheduling information you send us.
- **Optional Feedback:** exit-survey answers, testimonials, video recordings of testimonials (only if you opt in).

### b. Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** when you visit our website or use our service, we automatically collect basic technical information — browser type, device type, IP address, referring URL, pages visited, timestamps.
- **Cookies:** we use essential cookies (for shopping-cart and session functionality) and may use basic analytics cookies (to understand which pages get the most visits). We do not use advertising cookies or sell tracking data to third parties. See Section 7 for details.

### c. Information from Third Parties
- **Payment confirmation** from Stripe.
- **Call recording transcripts**, if applicable (only if you consent to recording at the start of your intake call — we will always ask).

We do **not** collect biometric data, location data beyond your IP-inferred general region, or special-category sensitive personal information (race, religion, health, etc.) unless you choose to share it as part of your brand story.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

- **Fulfilling your purchase:** producing the brand deliverables you ordered.
- **Communicating with you:** sending you order confirmations, scheduling intake calls, delivering your Outputs, responding to support requests, sending occasional service-related updates.
- **Improving the service:** learning from patterns across many customers to make our methodology and prompts better. We use anonymized or aggregated data for this — your individual story is not used as a "training example" for our AI models.
- **Legal and security purposes:** detecting fraud, enforcing our Terms, responding to lawful requests, defending legal claims.

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties.
- Use your data to train external AI models (our AI provider, Anthropic, contractually agrees not to train on commercial-tier data).
- Share your information with advertisers.
- Use your story or business details in marketing materials without your explicit written permission.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

- **Encryption in transit:** all data sent between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
- **Encryption at rest:** intake content and Outputs stored on our servers are encrypted.
- **Access controls:** only authorized personnel (currently Tab Wolod and the technical team operating the service) can access customer data, and only as needed to deliver the service.
- **Payment data:** handled by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified); we never store full card numbers.

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work hard to protect your information and will notify affected customers promptly if a breach occurs.
