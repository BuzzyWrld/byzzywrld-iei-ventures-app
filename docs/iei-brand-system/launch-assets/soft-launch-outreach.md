# Soft-Launch Outreach Templates

**Purpose:** Tab's warm-prospect DM/email templates for the first 20 outreach conversations. This is Stage 1 of the GTM doc — direct one-to-one sales before any paid ads, before any cohort offer, before any of it.

**Goal:** 3-5 paying customers from the first 20 warm outreaches.
**Conversion benchmark:** Warm-network DMs in Tab's space typically convert at 15-25% to a call, and 30-50% of calls convert to purchase. So 20 outreaches → 3-5 calls → 1-2 purchases is conservative; 3-5 is optimistic-realistic.

**Voice rules:** Per `BRAND_VOICE_SPEC.md` — conversational, no jargon, no "I hope this finds you well." Direct. Warm. Sign as Tab.

---

## Template 1 — THE DIRECT WARM ASK (best for close friends, past clients)

**Best for:** People you've worked with before, close friends, people who already know your work.
**Channel:** DM (IG, LinkedIn) or text.
**Length:** Under 80 words.

```
Hey [Name] — quick one.

Just launched Brand Blueprint — my IEI Brand System productized so founders can get the full brand foundation (messaging, logo, brand kit, 3-page website, GTM checklist) in 5 days, AI-built and reviewed by me personally. $997-$3,997 depending on what you need.

Three reasons I'm telling you specifically:
1. You [SPECIFIC THING — "have been talking about relaunching X" / "mentioned you were stuck on Y" / "I built your last brand 3 years ago and it's time"]
2. I'm only taking 6 customers a week — wanted to offer you first dibs
3. First 5 customers get a discount + I want your feedback to make this perfect

Want the link?

— Tab
```

**Customize the THREE reasons** — that's where the conversion happens. Generic = ignored. Specific = response.

## Template 4 — THE COLD-BUT-WARM (LinkedIn connection you've never spoken to)

**Best for:** People in your network you haven't talked to in years but who fit the ICA.
**Channel:** LinkedIn DM.
**Length:** Under 100 words.

```
Hey [Name] —

We've been LinkedIn connected for a while but haven't actually crossed paths. Hope you're well.

Saw your recent post on [SPECIFIC POST OR COMMENT — proves you actually looked]. Hit something for me — because what you described is exactly what I built Brand Blueprint to solve.

It's my IEI Brand System productized. Founders get a complete brand foundation in 5 days — messaging, logo, kit, 3-page website, GTM checklist. AI does the build, I review every line personally. $997-$3,997.

Tab
```

**Critical:** Reference an ACTUAL recent post of theirs. Cold without that signal reads spammy.

## Template 6 — THE FOLLOW-UP (3 days after no response)

**Best for:** Anyone you sent Template 1 or 2 to who didn't reply.
**Channel:** Same as original.
**Length:** Under 30 words.

```
Hey — no pressure. Just bumping in case the first message got buried. Want me to send the Brand Blueprint link, or not the right time? Either is cool.
```

That's it. Don't pile on. One follow-up. If no response after that, leave it. Move on.

## The 20-prospect list (build this BEFORE you send)

Before sending any of the above, build your list. Open a doc. Write down 20 names. For each:

| Field | What to capture |
|---|---|
| Name | Real name |
| Channel | IG / LinkedIn / email / text |
| Relationship | Past client / friend / network / cold |
| Why them | One sentence on why they're a fit |
| Template | Which of T1-T5 to use |
| Personalization hook | The SPECIFIC THING that goes in the message |
| Status | Not sent / Sent / Responded / Booked call / Purchased / Pass |

**Stop building the list at 20.** More than 20 = paralysis. Less than 20 = not enough top-of-funnel.

---

## The cadence (do this in 3 days, not 3 weeks)

**Day 1:** Build the 20-prospect list. Send 7 messages (the warmest 7 — past clients, close friends).
**Day 2:** Send next 7 messages (network you haven't talked to in a while). Post the IG story.
**Day 3:** Send last 6 messages (LinkedIn cold-but-warm). Reply to any responses from Day 1-2.

**Days 4-5:** Follow-ups (Template 6) to anyone who hasn't replied. Book intake calls for anyone who said yes.

**By end of Week 1:** Expect 4-7 responses, 2-4 booked intake calls, 1-2 paid customers in flight.

If you get fewer responses than that, the issue is almost always one of three things:
1. **List quality** — they weren't the right people. Rebuild the list.
2. **Personalization** — the "specific thing" was generic. Rewrite it.
3. **Offer clarity** — the message didn't say what they get. Tighten the templates.

## What to do when someone responds positively

Don't sell. Just book the call.

```
Yes — let's talk! Pick a 15-min slot here: [CALENDLY LINK]
On the call I'll explain how it works + answer any questions. No pressure, just info. Then you decide.
```

**Critical:** The free 15-min call is "is this right for me?" — NOT the 60-min intake call. The 60-min intake comes AFTER they've purchased. Don't confuse these two.

Set up a SECOND Calendly event for these 15-min discovery calls. Link it separately. Don't burn the 60-min intake slot on someone who hasn't paid.

---

## What to do when someone responds negatively

```
Totally get it. If you change your mind, you know where to find me. And if you know anyone who's stuck — would love the intro. Either way, appreciate you.
```

Done. Move on. Don't argue.

---

## What to do when someone says "tell me more first"

Don't write a novel in DM. Send the link + one specific thing:

```
Yeah — landing page has everything: ieiventures.com/blueprint

Here's the punchline: 5 days, full brand foundation (messaging, logo, kit, 3-page website, GTM checklist), AI does the build, I personally review every output before you see it. $997-$3,997 depending on which tier.

If after looking you want to talk it through — 15 min here: [CALENDLY DISCOVERY LINK]
```

Three sentences max. Let the landing page do the heavy lifting. Your DM job is to GET THEM TO THE PAGE, not to close the sale.
