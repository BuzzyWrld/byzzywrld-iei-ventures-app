# Tab Video Script — 90 Seconds (Landing Hero)

**Purpose:** The 90-second video at the top of the Brand Blueprint landing page. This is the single highest-leverage trust-building asset on the entire page.

**Tone:** Your real voice. Conversational. Direct. No "in today's fast-paced world" energy. Pretend you're talking to one founder, not a camera.

**Setup:**
- Record on phone or Loom — quality matters less than authenticity
- Decent lighting (window light or ring light)
- Plain wall or your actual workspace as backdrop
- Look at the camera, not the screen
- Don't script-read — internalize the beats, then talk
- One take is fine; "imperfect" reads as human, not amateur

**Length target:** 80-95 seconds. Anything over 2 minutes loses 50% of viewers.

---

## DELIVERY NOTES

**DO:**
- Smile early (within first 3 seconds). It changes the whole vibe.
- Pause briefly after "If you can't answer those five — your audience can't either." Let it land.
- Slow down on the offer pricing — that's where eyes glaze. Make it crisp: "Under a thousand bucks. Five days. Done."
- End with eye contact, not a wave.

**DON'T:**
- Don't read off a teleprompter — it always shows.
- Don't say "um" more than twice. If you do, just re-record. Re-takes are free.
- Don't ad-lib new pitches that aren't on the landing page. The video has to match what they read.
- Don't use jargon. If you catch yourself saying "ecosystem," "scalable," or "transformative" — start over.
