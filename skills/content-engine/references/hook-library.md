# Hook Library — High-Retention Structural Patterns

These are structural templates extracted from top-performing short-form content in the B2B,
SaaS, and digital marketing niches. The model must select 3 hook structures from this library
during the Market Analysis step (Step 1) and declare which 3 it is applying to the batch.

Hook selection rule: pick structures appropriate to the week's trending topics. Do not apply
the same structure to the same day type across weeks.

---

## Hook Structure 1 — The Hard Number Drop
**Pattern:** "[Exact number] [noun] [did something]. [Shocking result]."
**Example:** "73 agencies tried AI-only content in Q1. 68 killed their engagement rate."
**Best for:** Monday (Signal), Thursday (Autopsy), Saturday (Hitlist)
**Why it works:** Specificity signals authority. The brain parses numbers before words.

## Hook Structure 2 — The Verdict First
**Pattern:** "[Company/tool/strategy] [is/was/failed]. Here's the data."
**Example:** "Pepsi's 2024 rebrand failed. Here's the data."
**Best for:** Thursday (Autopsy), Monday (Signal)
**Why it works:** Eliminates suspense. Viewer stays for the proof, not the reveal.

## Hook Structure 3 — The Cost Statement
**Pattern:** "You're losing [specific amount/metric] because [single specific reason]."
**Example:** "You're losing 31% of your leads because your brand colors signal the wrong industry."
**Best for:** Monday (Signal), Wednesday (AI Paradox), Saturday (Hitlist)
**Why it works:** Loss aversion. Makes the cost of inaction immediate and personal.

## Hook Structure 4 — The Mechanism Reveal
**Pattern:** "[Popular result] isn't [common explanation]. It's [actual mechanism]."
**Example:** "Nike's brand equity isn't marketing spend. It's 40 years of consistent visual identity."
**Best for:** Wednesday (AI Paradox), Thursday (Autopsy)
**Why it works:** Contradicts a held belief. Forces a pattern interrupt.

## Hook Structure 5 — The Speed Proof
**Pattern:** "We gave [system/tool] [complex task]. It shipped in [specific time]."
**Example:** "We gave the IEI Dashboard a 12-page brief. It shipped a full brand kit in 4 minutes."
**Best for:** Friday (Sandbox)
**Why it works:** Credibility through specificity. A vague claim ("fast") is ignored. A timed claim lands.

## Hook Structure 6 — The Accumulation Frame
**Pattern:** "[Time period], [metric grew/compounded]. One [system/decision] caused it."
**Example:** "18 months. CAC dropped 40%. One brand infrastructure decision caused it."
**Best for:** Monday (Signal), Thursday (Autopsy), Sunday (Growth Engine)
**Why it works:** Compounding is aspirational. Attributing it to a single decision is actionable.

## Hook Structure 7 — The Direct Tag
**Pattern:** "@[Platform/Audience]: [specific problem statement]. [IEI has the fix]."
**Example:** "@Vendasta partners: you're shipping 12 deliverables per client. We cut it to 3."
**Best for:** Saturday (Hitlist)
**Why it works:** Named attention is impossible to scroll past. Specificity signals relevance.

## Hook Structure 8 — The Sequential Tease
**Pattern:** "Step [X] of 3: [single outcome delivered in this step]."
**Example:** "Step 1 of 3: How to generate your client's full brand kit before the discovery call ends."
**Best for:** Tuesday (Blueprint) — use for Part 1, Part 2, Part 3 sequentially
**Why it works:** Series commitment. Viewer has a reason to return for Part 2 and 3.

## Hook Structure 9 — The Affiliate Proof
**Pattern:** "[Name/role] was at [baseline]. [Time] later, at [new level]. System: [IEI]."
**Example:** "Sarah ran a 2-person design shop. 6 months later, 14 white-label clients. System: IEI."
**Best for:** Sunday (Growth Engine)
**Why it works:** Social proof with specificity. Named + numbered = credible.

## Hook Structure 10 — The Paradox Statement
**Pattern:** "[The thing everyone is doing] is why [the thing everyone wants] isn't happening."
**Example:** "Posting every day is why your brand isn't growing."
**Best for:** Wednesday (AI Paradox), Monday (Signal)
**Why it works:** Directly challenges behavior the ICA is currently engaged in. Forces reconsideration.

## Hook Selection Protocol (Step 1 — Market Analysis)

After completing trend analysis, select exactly 3 hook structures from this library that
align with the 3 trending topics you identified. Document the selection:

```
HOOK SELECTIONS FOR THIS BATCH:
Trending Topic 1: [topic] → Hook Structure [#]: [reason for fit]
Trending Topic 2: [topic] → Hook Structure [#]: [reason for fit]
Trending Topic 3: [topic] → Hook Structure [#]: [reason for fit]
```

Apply the selected structures as the primary hook formats for the week. The remaining 4 days
can use any remaining structures from the library, as long as no structure is used more than
twice across the 7 days of Week 1.
