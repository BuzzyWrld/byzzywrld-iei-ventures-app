# Tone Guard — Bold. Direct. Built.

This file is a vocabulary enforcement system. Run it as a mandatory audit pass on every asset
before finalizing. If any red-light word or pattern is found, rewrite before committing output.

---

## Green-Light Vocabulary

Default to these. If you find yourself reaching for a synonym, check the red-light list first.

**Verbs (actions IEI takes):**
Ship, Generate, Deploy, Install, Execute, Automate, Scale, Build, Extract, Eliminate, Drive,
Convert, Cut, Force, Trigger, Activate, Run, Compress, Compound, Engineer, Systematize, Fire,
Audit, Tear down, Reconstruct

**Nouns (what IEI produces):**
Infrastructure, System, Engine, Pipeline, Stack, Machine, Asset, Output, Deliverable,
Framework, Blueprint, Architecture, Dashboard, Signal

**Adjectives (how IEI describes quality):**
Locked-in, Proven, Measurable, Structured, Systematic, Documented, Repeatable, Exact

---

## Red-Light Vocabulary

Any of these appearing in a finalized asset is a defect. Rewrite immediately.

**Banned verbs:**
strive, leverage, elevate, unlock, empower, foster, nurture, navigate (when figurative),
showcase, underscore, delve, utilize (use "use" or "run" instead)

**Banned adjectives:**
comprehensive, robust, nuanced, multifaceted, vibrant, pivotal, seamless, cutting-edge,
innovative (as a vague descriptor), transformative, holistic, dynamic, scalable (as vague praise),
unparalleled, world-class, best-in-class, synergistic, disruptive

**Banned nouns:**
thought leader, thought leadership, ecosystem (when generic), synergy, tapestry, landscape
(when figurative), paradigm, interplay, journey (when describing a brand's path)

**Banned phrases (common AI filler):**
- "We strive to..."
- "We believe that..."
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "In an ever-evolving landscape..."
- "Navigate the complexities of..."
- "Unlock the potential of..."
- "Take your business to the next level"
- "Stand out from the crowd"
- "The future of X is here"
- "At our core..."
- "At the intersection of..."
- "Where X meets Y"
- "Game-changer"
- "It's important to note that..."
- Any sentence starting with "Moreover," "Furthermore," "Additionally,"

**Banned tone patterns:**
- Passive voice: "is designed to help" → "ships results"
- Hedged claims: "can potentially generate" → "generates"
- Throat-clearing openers: Any hook that does not have a payoff in the first 7 words
- "Thought leader" framing on LinkedIn: No "I've been thinking about..." openers

---

## Self-Audit Checklist (run before every asset is finalized)

For each asset, verify ALL of the following:

- [ ] No red-light words or phrases present anywhere
- [ ] First sentence of every asset leads with outcome or active verb — not context-setting
- [ ] No sentence is passive voice
- [ ] No sentence exceeds 20 words (break it if it does)
- [ ] The asset sounds like it was written by a builder, not a marketer
- [ ] Video hooks deliver the payoff in 3 seconds of reading — not 7
- [ ] LinkedIn posts open with a noun or number — not "I" and not a question
- [ ] GEO article H2/H3 headers use active verbs — "How to Deploy X" not "Understanding X"
- [ ] At least one specific data point, dollar figure, or measurable metric is cited per asset
- [ ] The IEI Dashboard / system / platform is referenced as the mechanism — not as an abstract capability

## Before/After Rewrites

Six examples. Pattern-match every asset against these.

**1. Service description**
❌ "Our platform is designed to help marketing teams streamline their workflow and achieve better results."
✅ "Deploy one platform. Cut your content workflow in half."

**2. Value proposition**
❌ "We strive to deliver innovative brand solutions that elevate your business."
✅ "We build brand infrastructure. Ship once. Run forever."

**3. Video hook**
❌ "Today I want to talk about why most marketing campaigns fail to deliver consistent ROI."
✅ "Your last campaign failed. Here's the exact data point that killed it."

**4. LinkedIn opener**
❌ "I've been thinking a lot lately about the role of AI in modern brand strategy..."
✅ "3 brands spent $2M on AI content last quarter. All 3 killed engagement. Here's the structural reason why."

**5. CTA**
❌ "If you're interested in learning more about how IEI can help your business grow, feel free to reach out."
✅ "Book a build session. Walk out with a system."

**6. Case study framing**
❌ "This is a great example of how brand consistency can have a transformative impact on customer perception."
✅ "Consistent brand identity compounds. This brand saw 34% higher recall with zero additional ad spend."
