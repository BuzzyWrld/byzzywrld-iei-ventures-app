# Exemplar Index — IEI Brand Playbooks

Seven reference playbooks spanning DTC, B2B, fintech, streetwear, wellness tech, sextech, media, and creative community. Each was authored at IEI's quality bar and is the source of truth for structure, depth, voice, and visual rigor.

Use this index to pick the closest 1–2 exemplars to a new client engagement. Read those in full before drafting.

---

## acetv.html

- **Brand:** AceTV+ — independent media platform / production house for film, journalism, and culture.
- **Tone/voice:** Empathetic but unsoft, "informed older sibling," receipts-first, anti-algorithm. Tagline: *"We're all we got."*
- **Visual aesthetic:** Dark editorial broadcast — Void Black `#0A0A0A`, Broadcast Red `#E8231A`, Press Gold `#F5A623`, Signal White warm. Space Grotesk + DM Sans + IBM Plex Mono. Feels like a streaming UI crossed with a media kit.
- **Best fit for:** Media / publishing / culture / creator-economy clients with a Gen Z–Millennial audience and an anti-establishment, truth-positioning angle. Brands building authority through content rather than product.
- **Standout structural moves:** Worksheet-numbered sections (WS2 / WS3 / WS4) acting as a working playbook, not a finished deck. Bios offered in 3 numbered IG options. Content Pillars as a **separate** card grid from Copy Pillars. Color palette annotated with audience/industry/psychology rationale per swatch.

## famfit.html

- **Brand:** FamFit — social fitness app pairing partner accountability with scalable workouts.
- **Tone/voice:** Warm, supportive, "reliable older sibling," motivating but never aggressive. Tagline: *"Get fit together."*
- **Visual aesthetic:** Bright millennial DTC wellness — Midnight Navy `#0F1923`, Fit Teal `#00C6A2`, Energy Amber `#F5A623`, Cloud White. Space Grotesk + DM Sans + IBM Plex Mono. Airy, breathable, 8px grid.
- **Best fit for:** Consumer wellness, fitness, habit, or accountability apps. Especially products that succeed on social proof and behavior change rather than feature depth. Uses an "Inconsistent Beginner" archetype that travels well to any habit-formation client.
- **Standout structural moves:** Section header pages with eyebrow + section number + descriptor sentence (TOC-style). 3 named tone pillars + 3 named voice pillars side by side. Three Offerings table with parallel "Key Features / Key Benefits" rows. Buying Triggers ranked 1–3, Channels ranked 1–4.

## wone.html

- **Brand:** Wone — financial infrastructure platform for climate tech founders.
- **Tone/voice:** Authoritative, transparent, direct. Sage-Magician hybrid — "senior partner who also founded two startups." Declarative sentences, no hedging. Tagline: *"Capital Finds Clarity."*
- **Visual aesthetic:** Navy-and-gold institutional fintech — Deep Midnight `#0A1A2E`, Deep Teal `#0D3B4C`, Clarity Teal `#00C9A7`, Muted Gold `#C9A227`, Warm Parchment. Space Grotesk + DM Sans, premium editorial gravitas.
- **Best fit for:** B2B fintech, infra, climate, deeptech, or any founder-facing platform that must signal institutional credibility AND innovation energy. Anywhere "trust + authority" is the brand job.
- **Standout structural moves:** Numbered Sections (01–07) with persistent top-nav breadcrumbs (Cover · Identity · Voice · Visual · Audience · Strategy). Elevator pitch in **two lengths** (one-sentence + three-sentence). Voice Guidelines with literal ✓ / ✗ example sentences. "We Are Not / We Are" category-definition block. Stat-driven hero (40% drop, 58% trust, 3× success).

## pen2purpose.html

- **Brand:** Pen2Purpose — community brand for writers (workshops, events, club).
- **Tone/voice:** Intentional not motivational, grounded not academic, warm not casual, sharp not clever. Like a good editor. Tagline variations: *"Write with intention. Share with purpose."* / *"The page is waiting."*
- **Visual aesthetic:** Dark literary salon — Deep Indigo `#0C0030`, Old Gold `#C4A35A`, Parchment `#F5F0E6`. **All-serif** type system: IM Fell English (display) + Libre Baskerville (body) + Cormorant Garamond (accent). Etching illustrations, gold rules, grain overlays, circular logo seal.
- **Best fit for:** Creative communities, literary/arts brands, education, salons, or any client where the visual identity must signal heritage, craft, and quiet authority — explicitly *not* tech-default sans. Use this when the client says "we don't want it to look like every other startup."
- **Standout structural moves:** "Visual Philosophy" paragraph **before** the color grid, explaining why each choice exists. **No sans-serif** stated as a brand law. Tagline Variations broken out **by use-case** (Primary / Campaign / Community / Workshop). Logo Rules in a clean Do / Don't two-column.

## banger.md

- **Brand:** Banger — consent + sexual wellness platform with digital records, STD verification, and video consent capture.
- **Tone/voice:** Liberator + Sage hybrid. "Protection is sexy" — bold, gender-neutral, mutual, never clinical, never preachy. Modern adults, modern tools.
- **Visual aesthetic:** Editorial dark sextech — Onyx Black `#0D0D0D`, Midnight Indigo `#1A1A2E`, Desire Red `#FF3B6B`, Safety Teal `#00C9A7`, Warm Ivory. Bebas Neue + Work Sans + DM Sans. "Dazed & Confused meets legal documentation."
- **Best fit for:** Sextech, intimacy, healthtech, legaltech, or any taboo / sensitive-category brand that needs to look premium and trustworthy without sterile clinical cues. Also strong for tier-architecture pricing models (Free → Premium → Moat tier).
- **Standout structural moves:** **Tier Architecture as the moat narrative** — each tier named (The Handshake / The Form / The Shield / The Record) and tagged with strategic role (Acquisition / Core Revenue / Growth Driver / The Moat). Three-competitor matrix with Direct / Associative / Partner-Competitor classification. Motion direction explicitly written ("a sealed envelope, not a confetti cannon").

## offscript.md

- **Brand:** Off Scr!pt. — NYC streetwear + community, 1-of-1 collab drops, ticketed events, Discord academy.
- **Tone/voice:** Bold, playful, culturally sharp, warm, unapologetic. Casual but intentional. "The cool older sibling who brought you to your first real event." Mantras: *be yourself · be new · be safe · be fearless.*
- **Visual aesthetic:** Off-Black + Off-White streetwear with three energy primaries — OS Red `#E8231A`, OS Cobalt `#2B5CE`, OS Yellow `#FFD060`. Poppins (display) + DejaVu Sans + DejaVu Mono. Bee mascot system in 6 colorways.
- **Best fit for:** Streetwear, drop-culture brands, IRL community products, or any founder-led brand whose business model is the **flywheel** (community → events → drops → community). Also great when the client has dual entities or co-founders with split roles.
- **Standout structural moves:** "The Money Engine" framed as 3 named revenue streams with reinvestment flywheel logic. Mantras list ("Be Yourself / Be New / Be Safe / Be Fearless") doubling as content-pillar series. Competitor table that names a specific "OS Edge" angle per competitor. Asset Inventory with ✓ / ⚠ / ○ status flags + a "Logo Evolution Flag" callout. Two-entity branding note.

## vent.md

- **Brand:** Vent — emotional-release audio app. Recordings self-delete in 30 seconds. No data, no storage, no audience.
- **Tone/voice:** The Liberator. Blunt · spare · unapologetic · truthful · dark but not nihilistic. Anti-wellness-culture, anti-attention-economy. Taglines: *"say it. lose it."* / *"the app designed to be forgotten."*
- **Visual aesthetic:** High-contrast minimalist tech — The Void `#080808`, Ember `#FF3C00`, Bone `#F0EDE8`. Bebas Neue + DM Sans + Space Mono. Refuses pastels, refuses purple/teal "wellness adjacency."
- **Best fit for:** Wellness/mental-health-adjacent products that explicitly want to **break** wellness-culture tropes. Privacy-first products. Single-feature apps where the feature *is* the brand promise. Ephemeral / anti-data positioning.
- **Standout structural moves:** "Three-axis" color justification (archetype × ICA × psychology) above the swatches. Brand Essence reduced to **one word** ("RELEASE"). VENT IS / VENT IS NOT voice table. Three-tier product naming that escalates the brand metaphor (The Void → Release → Ritual). Competitive moat written as a pull-quote, not a paragraph. Persona named "The Contained One" with trait-tag chips.

## How to use

1. **Read this INDEX.md first.**
2. Identify the client's industry, audience age band, tone, and aesthetic axis (light/dark, serif/sans, institutional/streetwear, premium/playful).
3. **Pick the 1–2 closest exemplars.** If the client is genuinely cross-genre (e.g. "premium wellness streetwear"), pick one for tone and one for structure.
4. **Read those exemplars in full** — don't skim. The structural moves and copy depth are the point.
5. **Then draft.** Mirror the structure (sections, numbering, table patterns, callout patterns) but never copy the brand-specific words. The exemplars teach the *grammar* of an IEI playbook, not the content.
