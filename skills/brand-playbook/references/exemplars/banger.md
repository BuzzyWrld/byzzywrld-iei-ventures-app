## SECTION ONE — BRAND IDENTITY

### 01 — Brand Overview

**Who We Are**
The world's first intimacy infrastructure platform — making consent as natural as connection.

> "Banger exists because the most intimate moments of human life deserve the most intentional protection. We make that protection effortless, beautiful, and mutual."

**THE ESSENCE — Mutual Protection, Zero Friction**
Banger sits at the intersection of intimacy and accountability — giving consenting adults the tools to protect each other legally, medically, and emotionally before, during, and after connection.

## SECTION TWO — VISUAL IDENTITY

### Color Palette

Derived from color psychology × sextech audience × brand personality. Audience signal overrides industry convention.

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Desire Red | `#FF3B6B` | Primary Accent. CTAs, logo, borders, emphasis. Neurologically signals attraction, urgency, intimacy. |
| Safety Teal | `#00C9A7` | Secondary Accent. Trust signals, verification badges, health tier features. Communicates safety, clarity, medicine. |
| Onyx Black | `#0D0D0D` | Primary Background. Creates sophistication and privacy — never pure black; always warm-dark. |
| Midnight Indigo | `#1A1A2E` | Secondary Background. Section dividers, card surfaces, depth layering. Adds intimacy without full darkness. |
| Warm Ivory | `#F0EDE8` | Primary Text. Warmer than white — approachable, human, softens the clinical edge of a safety product. |
| Parchment | `#F5F2EE` | Light Mode Background. Legal documents, premium tier materials. Signals trust, formality, legitimacy. |

**DO NOT USE**
No pure white (#FFFFFF) — use Warm Ivory. No neon green — conflicts with medical/STD association. No pastels — undercut the brand's authority. No purple — generic tech/app cliché. No orange — reads as warning, not desire.

**60-30-10 RULE:** Onyx/Midnight (60%) · Ivory/Parchment text (30%) · Desire Red + Safety Teal (10%).

### Typography System

Three typefaces, three roles. Never more, never less.

**DISPLAY — BEBAS NEUE — BOLD/400** — Headlines, logo, hero text.
> BANGER. CONSENT VERIFIED. PROTECTION HAS NEVER FELT THIS GOOD.

Bebas Neue · Display · 400 · Minimum 18px · All-caps only · Never body copy.

**BODY — WORK SANS — 300/400/500/600** — Paragraphs, descriptions, UI.
> Banger makes mutual consent the most natural part of intimacy. Both parties confirm, both parties are protected. It takes twelve seconds.
> Your records are encrypted, timestamped, and stored privately in your vault. We never see them. Neither does anyone else unless you choose to share.

Work Sans · Body · 300 (captions) / 400 (body) / 500 (labels) / 600 (emphasis) · Minimum 13px.

**ACCENT — DM SANS — 300/400/500** — Labels, eyebrows, UI tags, metadata.
> CONSENT VERIFIED · STATUS ACTIVE · TIMESTAMP 04:20:02 PM
> Tier: The Record · Jurisdiction: New York · Attorney-Reviewed ✓

DM Sans · Accent · 300/400/500 · Used for UI labels, tags, pills, eyebrows · Never for paragraphs.

**PAIRING RULES**
Bebas Neue never below 18px. Work Sans never above 600 weight for body copy. DM Sans stays in label/meta contexts only — never as headlines. Bebas + Work Sans is the core pairing for all hero and content sections. Never use a fourth typeface.

### Visual Language

Photography direction, iconography, texture, and spatial philosophy.

**PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION — Editorial, Not Clinical**
High contrast. Desaturated or warm-toned. Intimate but never explicit. No stock photo smiles. No sterile white backgrounds. Pairs of hands, silhouettes, architecture of privacy. Think: Dazed & Confused meets legal documentation.

**TEXTURE + PATTERN — Subtle Grain, No Noise**
Minimal grain overlay at 3–5% opacity on dark fields only. Gradient meshes using Desire Red → Midnight Indigo at 8–12% opacity. No loud textures, no brick/paper patterns — this is tech, not scrapbooking.

**ICONOGRAPHY — Line + Geometric**
Thin-line icons at 1.5–2px stroke weight. Clean geometric forms. Lock icons, checkmarks, shields, document glyphs — all redrawn in Banger's visual language. Never filled cartoon-style icons.

**SPACING PHILOSOPHY — Airy with Intent**
Generous negative space signals premium and private. Tight density reserved for legal document tiers only — where density signals thoroughness. UI components breathe. Minimum 24px padding in all card elements.

**MOTION DIRECTION — Animation Principles · Confident + Quiet**
Fade-and-rise on page load. No bounces, no playful wiggles. Confirmation animations: a single clean pulse on the consent button (Safety Teal glow, 300ms ease). Transition timing: 200–350ms standard, 500ms for tier reveals. The product never celebrates loudly — it confirms quietly. Like a sealed envelope, not a confetti cannon.

## SECTION THREE — PRODUCT OFFERINGS

### Tier Architecture

Four consumer tiers built for accessibility at entry and defensibility at premium.

**FREE — The Handshake (Acquisition)**
Mutual digital acknowledgment via unique session link. Both parties confirm. Timestamped confirmation sent to both emails. No account required. No friction. Entry point for viral adoption and platform trust-building.

**$1.99/mo — The Form (Core Revenue)**
PDF consent document with e-signature capture. Stored session record in personal encrypted vault. Downloadable certificate. Jurisdiction-aware language. Session history dashboard. The everyday standard for sexually active adults.

**$4.99/mo — The Shield (Growth Driver)**
Everything in The Form + STD status integration. Partners upload verified lab results from Everlywell, Quest Diagnostics, or LabCorp. Results display as verified badge visible to consenting partner only. Results auto-expire at 90 days. Health history dashboard included.

**$9.99/mo — The Record (The Moat)**
Everything in The Shield + timestamped video consent capture. Both parties appear on camera, verbally confirm agreement. Encrypted, stored, legally significant artifact. Attorney-reviewed language per US jurisdiction. The strongest legal artifact available to private individuals in consent documentation. This is the product moat.

**FUTURE TIER — B2B / Enterprise · Institutional Licensing**
University Title IX offices, HR departments, sexual health clinics, legal aid organizations. White-label API, compliance reporting dashboard, volume licensing. Pricing: $499–$2,499/mo depending on seat count. *[Recommended — confirm with client after MVP launch]*

## SECTION FIVE — COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

### Competitor Analysis

No direct competitor owns this space. Banger competes in three adjacent categories simultaneously.

**01 LegalFling — Direct Competitor (Defunct/Dormant) · Blockchain-based consent app, 2018**
- *Strengths:* First mover. Generated significant press. Identified the real market need early.
- *Weaknesses / Banger's Opportunity:* Ugly UX. Blockchain gimmick over utility. No STD integration. Legally contested. No video capture. Died on friction. Banger wins by being everything LegalFling failed to be.

**02 DocuSign — Associative Competitor · Digital Signature / Legal Infrastructure**
- *Strengths:* Universal brand trust. Legal defensibility. Infrastructure. Banger benefits from DocuSign's category education — users already understand e-signatures.
- *Weaknesses / Banger's Opportunity:* Not built for intimacy. Clinical, corporate, zero personality. Would never solve the STD or video capture problem. Banger does everything DocuSign doesn't — for the bedroom.

**03 Everlywell / LabCorp — Associative Partner/Competitor · At-Home STD Testing**
- *Strengths:* Established health testing infrastructure. Consumer trust. Lab result credibility. Banger's Shield and Record tiers depend on their results as the verification source.
- *Weaknesses / Banger's Opportunity:* No consent layer. No sharing infrastructure. No legal record. These are testing companies, not intimacy platforms. Banger is the bridge between their data and personal protection.

- **Non-Monogamous + Lifestyle Communities** — Polyamorous, ENM, kink, and swinger communities have the highest consent literacy and the most acute need for documentation. These communities are also tight-knit and influential — a viral win here spreads fast.

## SECTION EIGHT — GO-TO-MARKET ROADMAP

### 3-Phase Launch Roadmap

From MVP to moat — sequenced for lean validation, earned growth, and defensible scale.

**PHASE 01 — VALIDATE · Month 1–3**
- Launch waitlist landing page (banger.app) with email capture and viral "send to someone" mechanic
- Ship MVP: The Handshake (free) + The Form ($1.99/mo) on iOS and Android
- Seed 500 beta users from personal networks, dating app communities, and lifestyle media
- Publish founding story and cultural positioning piece on Medium / Substack — news-peg to current consent events
- Establish TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter/X presence — content strategy: "consent is the new foreplay"
- Reach 1,000 active users; collect testimonials for press kit

**PHASE 02 — GROW · Month 4–8**
- Launch The Shield ($4.99/mo) with Everlywell and LabCorp result integration
- Launch partnership conversations with Hinge, Bumble, and Feeld for profile badge integration
- Pitch PR: TechCrunch, Vice, Vox, Rolling Stone — leverage consent news cycle hook
- Launch campus ambassador program at 5 major universities
- Test paid social on Meta and TikTok — targeting: dating app users aged 24–38
- Begin attorney partnership for jurisdiction-specific legal language (The Record prep)

**PHASE 03 — SCALE · Month 9–12**
- Launch The Record ($9.99/mo) — video capture with attorney-reviewed language in 5 US jurisdictions
- Launch B2B / Enterprise tier for universities and HR departments
- Begin acquisition or seed round conversations — target: Match Group, Bumble, LegalZoom, or sexual health VCs
- International expansion roadmap: UK, Canada, Australia (Phase 4)
- Publish annual Consent & Intimacy Report — owned media / PR asset
- Reach $50K MRR or 50K registered users — acquisition-ready milestone
