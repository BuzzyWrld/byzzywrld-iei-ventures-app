## Brand Style Guide

Color, typography, logo system, and visual rules.

### The Color System

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Off-Black | `#0A0A0A` | Primary field |
| Off-White | `#F5F5F0` | Primary text |
| OS Red | `#E8231A` | Energy / Identity |
| OS Cobalt | `#2B5CE` (cobalt blue) | Innovation / Drops |
| OS Yellow | `#FFD060` | Warmth / Community |
| Surface | `#1E1E1E` | Cards / panels |

**OS Red** — Energy, urgency, passion — commands attention. Bold primaries signal cultural heat in streetwear/lifestyle. Gen Z and millennial fit: high energy primaries. Usage: Patches, primary CTAs, hero accents. Never body text.

**OS Cobalt** — Innovation, confidence, digital-native energy. Stands out vs. yellow/red streetwear. Near-normal — signals "new era." Creative-class millennials + digital-native Gen Z. Usage: Drop announcements, Discord, community channels.

**OS Yellow** — Warmth, optimism, approachability, joy. Rare in dark-field streetwear. Signals welcome — invites people in. Usage: Mascot colorway, community warmth moments.

---

### Typography System

**DISPLAY / HEADING FONT — Poppins**
> Be Off Script. Drop No. 1 — Coming Soon.

- Weights: 300 / 400 / 500 / 700
- Role: Headlines, drop titles, section headers, cover pages
- Min size: 16px — never use below this
- Note: Warm, rounded confidence — matches brand personality exactly

**BODY FONT — DejaVu Sans**
> We build community first. Everything else follows. Join the drop, come to the event, wear what you believe.

- Role: Body copy, descriptions, captions
- Min size: 11px · Line height: 1.5

**ACCENT / LABEL FONT — DejaVu Mono**
> DROP 001 · LIMITED RUN · OS COMMUNITY

- Role: Eyebrows, labels, drop numbers, ticket codes
- Case: Always uppercase, always tracked
- Max size: never headlines

**PAIRING RULES**
- ✓ Poppins headline + DejaVu Sans body — always safe
- ✓ Poppins + Mono eyebrow — strong visual hierarchy
- ✓ All three on one layout — only if each has a distinct role
- ✕ Never use Poppins below 16px
- ✕ Never use Mono for emotional copy or storytelling
- ✕ Never mix Poppins with any serif — breaks brand personality

## Go-to-Market

Revenue model, SMART goals, market position.

### The Money Engine — Three Streams

**Stream 01 — 1-of-1 Drops · APPAREL**
10 samples per line. Collab drop — every piece is 1-of-1. Launch at $250. Unsold inventory re-listed on resale platforms at $500. Scarcity is the pricing mechanism.

**Stream 02 — Events + Tickets · COMMUNITY**
Community-led events with ticketed entry. Revenue reinvested to fund bigger, better events. The flywheel: events → revenue → better events → more community → more drops.

**Stream 03 — Academy + Digital · RYAN'S ENTITY**
Off Script Academy, affiliate hub, memberships, digital downloads, subscriptions. 100% owned by Ryan. Runs on Discord infrastructure.

---

### SMART Goals — Year One

**GOAL 01 — Drop Revenue**
Execute 3 collab drops, sell 90%+ within 14 days of each launch.
*Metric: $2,500–$3,000 net/drop after COGS · Timeline: Q1–Q3*

**GOAL 02 — Community**
Grow Discord to 500 active members by M6, 40%+ engagement on drop announcements.
*Metric: Member count + message activity*

**GOAL 03 — Events**
Host 4 ticketed events that break even or better, each larger than the last.
*Metric: Ticket revenue ≥ venue cost + 20% reinvestment buffer*

**GOAL 04 — Instagram**
Reach 2,000 Instagram followers by M6, 5%+ engagement rate.
*Metric: Followers + avg. likes per post*

**COMPETITOR 03 — Aimé Leon Dore** *(NYC boutique lifestyle)* *[Recommended — confirm competitors with client]*

**The OS Edge**
None of these competitors have the Community → Events → Drop flywheel at the early-stage, founder-led level. Off Script is not trying to be Supreme at scale. It's building what Supreme, Madhappy, and ALD used to be when the founders still knew every customer's name.

## Products + Niches

### Product Positioning — Three Offerings

**OFFERING 01 — 1-of-1 Collab Drops** · Apparel · Physical goods
- Price: $250 launch / $500 resale
- Run: 10 pieces per drop, all 1-of-1
- Pain solved: ICA wants to own something with a story that isn't mass-produced
- Value prop: Own verified 1-of-1 pieces before they're worth 2× on resale

**OFFERING 02 — Ticketed Events** · Community · IRL experiences
- Price: Variable by event scale
- Run: 4+ events/year, each bigger than last
- Pain solved: ICA wants community that isn't just digital
- Value prop: Ticket revenue funds experiences that make the next event unmissable

**OFFERING 03 — Off Script Academy** · Digital · Community membership
- Price: Subscription / course-based
- Platform: Discord + digital delivery
- Pain solved: ICA wants to learn and grow, not just buy merch
- Value prop: Community members stay, grow, and bring others in

### Niche Segments

- **NYC Streetwear Kids (18–25)** — first-gen culture kids who find brands through friends not ads. Entry via events.
- **Resale / Collectors** — Understands 1-of-1 logic. Buys at $250 expecting $500+ flip. Built-in amplifier.
- **Discord Communities** — Already on Discord. Academy channels pull this segment in naturally. Low CAC.
- **Collab Partners** — Small brands wanting OS community co-sign. Each collab is also a distribution play.
