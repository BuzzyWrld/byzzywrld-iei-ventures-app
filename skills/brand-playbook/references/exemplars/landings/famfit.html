How It Works

Three steps to
showing up.

No gym required. No complicated setup. Just you, your partner, and a shared commitment.

Step 01

Pair with someone

Invite a friend, sibling, partner, or parent. They join, you're linked. Your workouts are now connected — whether you're in the same city or across the country.

Step 02

Log your workouts

Mark a workout done. Your partner gets notified. They log theirs. The streak builds — and the moment one person skips, the other feels it. That's the accountability engine.

Step 03

Build the streak

Shared streaks, challenges, and milestones make every win feel twice as good. Progress isn't just tracked — it's celebrated between people who actually care.

Core Features

Built for people,
not performance.

FamFit isn't built for elite athletes. It's built for real people who want to feel healthy and stay connected.

1:1 Partner Pairing
Private accountability between exactly two people. Not a crowd. Not a leaderboard. Just you and someone you trust.
Streak Tracking
Visual streaks that make skipping feel genuinely bad — because letting yourself down is one thing, letting your partner down is another.
Scalable Workout Plans
Beginner-friendly routines that adapt to each partner's level. Work out together even when your fitness levels are miles apart.
Accountability Challenges
Weekly challenges designed for pairs. Friendly competition that keeps the relationship active, not just the workouts.
